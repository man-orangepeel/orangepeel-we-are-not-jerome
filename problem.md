# The Problem

Bitcoin parents criticize monetary printing — then give their kids unconditional fiat allowance every month. No effort. No value created. Jerome Powell at home.

---

## Existing tools fail in two ways

**They replicate the same model.**
Apps like Greenlight or Pixpay are just digital banks for kids. Bank custody, subscriptions, no real financial education. The underlying mechanic is unchanged: money arrives without effort.

**The Bitcoin education space hits the same wall.**
Every "Bitcoin for kids" app faces the same unsolved question: *who funds the rewards?* Most either require parents to constantly top up a custodial account, or rely on app tokens with no real value.

---

## The real problem

Parents already pay for their kids. The issue isn't the money — it's the flow.

Right now: parent works → earns fiat → bank → kid's account. No signal. No effort required from the child. No lesson learned.

**We don't need a new budget. We need to fix the flow.**
